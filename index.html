About <CODE O>

<CODE O> is the official coding community of the Computer Science & Engineering (AI & ML) branch at Malnad College of Engineering. This community is exclusive to students of our branch, focused on creating a strong, skilled, and innovative network within our cohort.

What We Do

  • Mini Hackathons: Hands-on coding competitions to solve real problems in limited time.
  • Ideathons: Brainstorm and develop innovative ideas into actionable projects.
  • Startup Talks: Sessions with startup founders and industry experts sharing knowledge and experience.
  • Workshops & Tutorials: Learn latest technologies and tools to sharpen your skills.
  • Peer Programming & Mentorship: Collaborate and grow together with fellow members.
  • Community Projects: Build projects together to add real experience to your portfolio.

Benefits for Members

  • Exclusive access to branch-focused coding challenges and events.
  • Networking opportunities with seniors, alumni, and industry professionals.
  • Personal growth through mentorship and collaboration.
  • Early insights into AI & ML trends and applications.
  • Boost your resume and coding portfolio with real projects.
  • Chance to represent our branch in inter-college coding events.
